ABOUT MRI IMAGE DENOISING

Noise is the unwanted energy which is generally intervened during the acquisition, transmission, and/or reconstruction of an image. Though the noise cannot be altogether eliminated, however, it can be reduced at acquisition time. It is an inexpensive way for the appointment booking, doctor referring, and image uploading for the lab.
